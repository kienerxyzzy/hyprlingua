Built withsimplicity_in mind.==>

Hypr directly compiles to HyprASM, a form of assembly optimized for VMs, allowing for high-speed computation.

Hypr has a simple, intuitive syntax for newcomers.
And if you ever wanted to revert to Hypr 1.0-style syntax, add #legacy10 to the top of your code.

The source code of Hypr is completely open-source, and *mostly* split into multiple files for readablity.
